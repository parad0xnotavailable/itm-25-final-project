  • For reservations of 8 guests or more, kindly contact us directly at 0917 001 5678.

  • To ensure the best possible dining experience, groups of 10 or larger will be required to do advanced orders. This helps our team prepare and provide better service for your party.
  • Please place all advanced orders at least 48 hours in advance. Our team will gladly assist you through our menu and any special requests you may have.
